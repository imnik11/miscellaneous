Subject : Invitation letter for technical festival
Respected Sir/Ma’am,
We are honoured to inform you about the National level Technical festival TantraFiesta 2019 organized by IIIT Nagpur. The event would showcase the talent in the fields of robotics, algorithm, science, technology, innovation, entrepreneurship, gaming etc. It is a platform to encourage healthy competition among the budding engineers of our country. The theme of our fest is Conquering the space. It aims at enlacing the trending skills, experiencing the real world and evolving to get best out of the individual. On behalf of TantraFiesta 2019 team, we take this honour to invite your college students to participate in this two days event from 28 th -29 th September 2019. We request you to encourage your students for participation.
We present you the most exciting events for the year:
  • Coddiction : A coding competition for enthusiastic geeks who would like to challenge their abilities.
  • Karyaneeti : A competition for brilliant minds to challenge their thinking ability to the next level and win exciting prizes.
  • Electrogiest : Electrogiest lets you prove yourself an Electronics-Geek. Be amongst the brilliants to win this electrifying competition.
Place: Nagpur
Yours faithfully,
Team TantraFiesta 2019
